Pixaurora Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

Pixaurora respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects user information while using our document scanner and PDF tools.

Information We Collect

Photos and Files

Pixaurora allows users to select images and PDF files from their device for:

  • Image to PDF conversion
  • PDF compression
  • Document scanning
  • OCR text recognition
  • Image editing and processing

Files are processed locally on the device. We do not upload personal files to our servers.

Camera Access

Pixaurora requests camera permission to scan documents and capture images directly inside the app.

Advertising and Analytics

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • App interaction data

This information may be used for:

  • Advertising
  • Analytics
  • Improving ad performance

Learn more: Google Advertising Policies

Third-Party Services

Pixaurora may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google ML Kit
  • Android Camera and Media APIs

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Permissions Used

  • Internet permission – for ads and online services
  • Camera permission – document scanning
  • Storage/media permission – selecting images and PDF files
  • Vibration permission – user feedback

Children's Privacy

Pixaurora is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Data Security

We use standard security practices and encrypted communication where applicable.

User Control

  • Users can delete generated files anytime
  • Permissions can be revoked from Android Settings
  • Users may clear app data or uninstall the app at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the feedback option available inside the app.
