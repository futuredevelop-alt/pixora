# Privacy Policy – Pixaurora Effective Date: May 29, 2026 Pixaurora ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects user information. ## Information We Collect ### Photos and Files Pixaurora allows users to select images and PDF files from their device storage for features such as: * Image to PDF conversion * PDF compression * Document scanning * OCR text recognition * Image editing and processing These files are processed locally on the user's device. We do not upload your files to our servers. ### Camera Access Pixaurora requests camera permission to allow users to scan documents and capture images directly within the app. ### Advertising ID and Device Information Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect: * Advertising ID * Device information * App interaction data This information may be used for: * Advertising * Analytics * Improving ad performance For more information, please review: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads ## Data Processing Most image and document processing happens locally on your device. We do not permanently store your personal files on external servers. ## Third-Party Services Pixaurora may use third-party services including: * Google AdMob * Google ML Kit * Android Media and Camera APIs These services may collect limited technical information as governed by their own privacy policies. Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy ## Permissions Used Pixaurora may request the following permissions: * Internet permission (for ads and online services) * Camera permission (document scanning) * Storage/media permission (selecting images and PDF files) * Vibration permission (user feedback) ## Children's Privacy Pixaurora is not directed toward children under 13 years of age. ## Data Security We use industry-standard security practices and encrypted network communication where applicable. ## User Control Users can: * Delete generated files anytime * Revoke permissions from Android Settings * Clear app data or uninstall the app at any time ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page. ## Contact Us If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through the feedback option available in the app.
